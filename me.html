General Info

I was born on March 9th 1973 in Chelan Washington. I was raised in Manson Washington, and when I was 17, my family and I moved to Provo, UT, USA, where I started my senior year at Timpview High School. (That is where I met Jon)
I have been a member of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints my whole life.
After high school, I enrolled at ITT Technical Institute in the Electrical Engineering Technology department. I did that for 2 semesters, and then went on a mission for my church. I spent 2 years in the Connecticut Hartford Mission, and served in 3 different states (CT, RI, and NY). I loved the experience, and if you want more information about my mission (or mission's in general), e-mail me.
I got home in '94, and enrolled at Utah Valley State College. I was double majoring in Electronics and Computer Technology, and Computer Science, but I put that on hold because of the job.
Needless to say, I am a computer nerd (NOT a computer geek), and proud of it. I have done this web page with Mozilla Composer and EditPad.
