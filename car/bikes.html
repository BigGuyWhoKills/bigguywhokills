Bikes

My two roomates each have Honda Shadow motorcycles.  No big deal right?  Well, one day Cliff let me ride his 1100cc Shadow Sabre, and now I have to own one of my own.  This is really one of those things that you will never really appreciate until you take even a short cruise around town.  That's all that I did on Cliff's bike (while he rode Marcus' 1800cc VTX), and now I can't wait to buy one.

Right now I am considering 3 brands: Harley Davidson, Honda, and Yamaha.  I'd love a Harley because it's American made and a legend, but they run about 50% more than a Japanese bike.  I would like a Honda because I've riden Cliff's, it rides nice, and has as much power as I'll ever need.  And I'd like a Yamaha because our family has owned Yamaha for many years, and I think their engines look the best of all the cruisers.  My next step is to take a few test rides, to see if there are any ride differences that I should be aware of.  This will also be the best way to test the torque and power of the engines.
