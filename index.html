I love music too...

To get files, I use µTorrent (Micro Torrent), a BitTorrent client.  In the past I've used Azureus, but it's kinda bloatware now.  Suprnova.org was the best Torrent site out there, but it was shut down.  LokiTorrent was also shut down.  ISO Hunt is about to be shut down as well.  TorrentReactor is another great site, but it's not very up to date.  The Pirate Bay is the best current site, but often changes domains.  KickassTorrents is what I use when TPB is down.
MP3.com is a good source for music news and MP3 hardware reviews.
