These are some of the movies that I own and want to own.  My preferences for movies are:

  1. Blu-Ray, I only buy DVDs if the Blu-Ray version is not available
  2. Widescreen (16x9), I never buy fullscreen unless it's the only version available (can you even get 4:3 movies anymore?)
  3. any special edition, directors cut, or unique version of the film
