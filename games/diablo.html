First some files.

Diablo First Aid, a decent multiplayer backup. Note: This is my favorite backup program, but these other two are very good too (That's why I use all three).

Another couple of good backup programs are: Diablo Backup and Diablo Multiplayer Backup. (Note: these are all MULTIPLAYER backup programs. They will NOT backup offline characters). Diablo Backup has great documentation, and lots of features. Diablo Multiplayer Backup creates only one file, but accepts command line parameters. (I use it to automatically backup my players by putting it in my 'startup' folder on my start menu in Windows.)

I am sick of listening to people ask where the multiplayer files are stored. They are in your windows directory, and are named dlinfo#.drv (where # is a number from 0-9). Diablo also makes some entries in your system registry, so use a program, don't do it yourself unless you know what you're doing!

If you are interested, here is the font used in Diablo (38k). If you have this font installed on your system, then this page will look VERY different. Allot of other pages have this font, but I added a readme file with instructions and the font name, as seen from documents, Exocet.
