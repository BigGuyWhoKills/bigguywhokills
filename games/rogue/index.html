Rainbow 6 and Rogue Spear

This page is devoted to my current favorite games, Rainbow Six and it's sequels: Rogue Spear, Ghost Recon, and Raven Shield.  Mostly it's a bunch of links that I frequent to get info, patches, and modifications, but I have pictures up here for eye-candy.
E-mail me if you have the need.

I spend a lot of my free time playing Raven Shield.
I started the demo on a GeForce 2 MX with an AMD Athlon 1333 on my Asus A7V266E mainboard with 256 Meg of PC2100 DDR.
My next rig was an Athlon XP 2600+, with 1 Gig of PC3200 DDR (dual channel) and a MSI GeForce 4 TI 4200 with 128 Meg of DDR.
That let me run in 1024x768 with all settings on high, rather than 800x600 (@ medium quality).  I could still keep my average FPS above 40 FPS (most maps) with that config, whereas before it used to be at about 30 FPS.
Then I had an Athlon 64 3500+ (socket 939) on an Abit AV8 mainboard with 1 Gig of PC3200 and a PNY GeForce 6800 GT with 256 Meg of DDR3.  This let me run Raven Shield at 1280x1024 and keep my average framerate always over 60.
Now I've got an Athlon 64 X2 4200+ on an Asus A8N-SLI Premium mainboard with 2 Gig of OCZ PC3200 2-3-2-5 and an nVidia GeForce 7800 GTX with 256 Meg of DDR3.  This supercedes any requirements that the RvS team could have planned for, so my FPS is probably off the chart.
