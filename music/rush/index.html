If you are here, then you probably know that Rush has had a big impact on my life. Easily my favourite (the 'u' is in honour of Geddy) group for many years now, I can sit down and listen to a disk and completely change my mood. Few other artists can do that, and none do it as well as Rush!
There are a lot of sites on the WWW with plenty of resources for Rush fans, and I have no plan of competing with them (I don't have the time or patience), but I figure that with as much as I love this group I ought to have a simple page of links (mostly for my own convenience, heheheh).
First off, I'm gonna start with the official web site: Rush.com, then the label website at Atlantic records, and a discography here on my own page. The original document was on the Rush/NMS site. Unfortunately, this site was closed in September of 1999. The National Midnight Star was my favourite Rush web page.

Two of my favourite Rush web sites are Niel's page and Geddy's page. I'm not afraid to say that I love these two for the unmeasurable amount of joy that they have poured into my life! Geddy has (had) a seperate page for his solo album, My Favourite Headache. - Update, seems Geddy has let that page go. Visit GeddyLee.net instead.

For a large and updated list of Rush links, try Flora's Music Room.
