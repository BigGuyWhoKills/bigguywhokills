WinAmp is THE BEST MP3 player!

What is an MP3 you say?  MP3 is an old compressed audio format used by grandparents and coal miners.  You should all be using a next generation compressed audio format like AAC or OGG.  If you are still making mp3's, shame on you!

I have been into MP3's since long before most people I knew even had a computer.  I vividly remember how excited I was when I got my first 40 Meg hard drive.  I was finally able to keep AN ENTIRE SONG on my computer (in PCM wave format)!  When I learned about MP3's I was excited beyond belief.  Then I could keep a few albums on my HUGE 200 Meg drive!  I now (2004) have more MP3's than I can easily listen to.  Other people have more MP3's, but I don't download them for the numbers, I download what I like to listen to.  I also use MP3's as a method to find and test new bands.

Support pro-MP3 bands like Limp Bizkit, Moby, and The Offspring!

The coolest thing about MP3's are the incredible number of B-sides and unreleased songs that can be found on the internet and downloaded for free.  Pearl Jam announced that they would allow the distribution of all of their non-album songs via the internet.  There are also a lot of songs out there that are album tracks and are illegal for you to listen to unless you own the album that they originated from.  As you can guess, this leads to a large amount of chatter as to the legality of some of the larger online archives.  I now use Azureus, a BitTorrent client to get new songs.

I bought a guitar on 15 Jan 2002, and now I spend my spare time practicing, and sometimes playing guitar tabs. Here are a few good sources for guitar:

    • Pitchfork has your hipster news and reviews.
    • Live Daily is a great source for all things musical.
    • Power Tab RULES!!!! - If you've ever touched a guitar, you need this software!
    • The Power Tab Archive RULES!!!! - If you've ever used Power Tab, you need to go here!
    • TabCrawler is another great tab site.    It's kinda slow, but has many versions.
    • For just one version of each song, goto the OnLine Guitar Archive.    They strive to have only one version, but a correct version.
    • Tabcrawler has a section on guitar instructions.    If you play, then should check it out.
    • I also check out MX Tabs.
    • And a list of easy to play guitar tabs at About.com.
