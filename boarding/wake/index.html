Snowboarding and Wakeboarding


To start off, buy a Hyperlite Premier.  You will never regret this decision.

I've been snowboarding since 1998. My nephew, Ryan, has been snowboarding since 2000. That little punk is already way better than I am. Ryan, Jason, and I all started wakeboarding this year (2001), and we are still about at the same level. My brother, Tom, started wakeboarding 2 years ago, but hasn't practiced much until this year. Now Tom has a bit more rope time, and he is better than the rest of us at some things, but Ryan can get the most air.
